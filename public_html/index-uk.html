Md Search is wholesaler of exclusive scarves trading under the brand name "HIGHFIELD".
Since 15 years MD Search has presented a summer and a winter collection.

This collection consists of knitted-, woven- and printed scarves in wool, silk, cotton and/or a mix of the best quality fabrics. We only choose the best quality yarns and this is reflected in the feel, drape and life span of our products.
Our customers are boutiques, lifestyle stores, better specialty and fashion stores.
In addition we also develop and produce for some well known high street stores under their own label.

If you are interested in our collections, call us. We are always happy to help answer any questions and maybe make an appointment in our showroom or at your location.
